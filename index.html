Summary

I build small, practical apps that turn supply-chain questions into data-driven answers: late-risk & ETA prediction, inventory policy (Safety Stock / ROP / EOQ), routing, and supplier risk. Everything here is open-source, fast to try, and focused on business impact.

Why this site?

  • Show, don’t tell: live demos you can click, not slides.
  • Open stack: Python, scikit-learn, Streamlit, Plotly—no paid APIs.
  • Decision-ready: each demo states the problem, method, and what to do next.

Featured demo — ETA & Reorder Advisor

Problem: Stockouts and expediting because supplier delays aren’t predicted and ROPs are static.
Solution: Learn from shipment history to predict late-risk & ETA; compute Safety Stock & ROP/EOQ for chosen service levels.
Tools: Python · Streamlit · scikit-learn · pandas · numpy · plotly.
